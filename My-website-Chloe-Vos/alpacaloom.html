THE ALPACA LOOM

The Alpaca Loom is such a cute place to visit when you are looking for something different to do.This is a fun and entertaining experience to be able to feed the alpacas and see them play and even fight with eachother. A packet of food to feed the llamas are about R20, which in my opinion isn't a lot. I also enjoyed the Cafe's hot chocolate and alpaca themed cupcakes they served. It is definitely a family friendly outing and can even be a nice scenery for photoshoots. I totally recommend!

★★★★☆
