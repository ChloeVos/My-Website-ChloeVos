CLIFTON 4TH BEACH

Clifton 4th Beach is the perfect place to go on a hot summer's days with your friends and family.The views are breathtaking thanks to the sparkely blue waters, white sand and beautiful mountains surrounding the area. This provides ample oppurtunities to take stunning photos of the scenery.There are security cameras and lifeguards ensuring everyone's safety. Refreshments are also available as there are friendly gentlemen who go around selling ice-lollies and sodas.

★★★★★
