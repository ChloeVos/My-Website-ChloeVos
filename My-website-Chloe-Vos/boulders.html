BOULDERS BEACH

Boulders Beach is one extra special beach in Cape Town and personally one of my favorites. Boulders Beach is a popular family friendly beach where the kids could play on the rocks and swim all day long. The reason I love Boulders Beach so much is because of the private and secluded feel it gives. It boasts with the best backdrop for photos and it is very special to be so closes to the penguins. The entry fee is R65 and totally worth it.

★★★★★
